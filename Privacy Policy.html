Introduction

Welcome to Vaulty! This Privacy Policy explains how your information is handled when you use the app on an Apple Watch.

Data Collection

Vaulty does not collect, store, or share any personal or user data. All accounts and notes are stored securely on, and only on, your Apple Watch using Apple's Keychain services. Vaulty does not transmit any data to external servers or third-party services.

Data Security

Vaulty uses robust encryption methods to ensure that your saved data is protected. All data remains on your device and is inaccessible to the developer or any external entities.

Third-Party Services

Vaulty does not integrate with any third-party services that collect user data. All functionalities are handled locally on Apple Watch.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time and as needed. Any changes will be reflected on this page, and the effective date will be updated accordingly. Your are encouraged to review this Privacy Policy periodically.
